# 📚 My Favorite Tutorials

Welcome to **My Favorite Tutorials** — a community-driven collection of resources, tips, and guides to help students successfully complete their **IBM certifications** through the Holberton x IBM partnership! 🎓

Whether you're just getting started or looking for advice on a specific course, you're in the right place. Let's learn together! 🚀

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This project aims to:

- Help students navigate the IBM SkillsBuild and Skills Network platforms
- Share useful resources for each IBM certification course
- Collect tips and tricks from students who already passed the certifications
- Build a supportive open source community around learning

---

## 📋 Certifications Covered

| Certificate | Platform | Difficulty | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Introduction to Open Source | IBM Skills Network | ⭐ Beginner | ~4 hours |
| Agile Explorer | IBM SkillsBuild | ⭐ Beginner | ~1 day |

## 💡 Tips to Succeed

- ✅ Create your IBM accounts **before** clicking any course links
- ✅ Always log in with your **@holbertonstudents.com** email
- ✅ Select **"Apprenants adultes"** when asked for your learner type
- ✅ Log in to **both** platforms first (SkillsBuild & Skills Network)
- ✅ Take notes during the courses — the quizzes are based on the content!
- ✅ Don't skip the intermediate quizzes — they prepare you for the final exam
